Nolan Wagener

I am a final-year PhD candidate in robotics at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where I am advised by Byron Boots (UW) and Panagiotis Tsiotras. I am interested in learning for robotics, particularly in allowing robots to safely and efficiently learn tasks. I am fortunate to have been funded by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and received and been nominated for several "best paper" awards at robotics conferences.

I am currently in the industry job market for a position in cutting-edge research in robotics and machine learning.
